# Customer Acquisition Strategies

## Acquisition Channels Overview

### Paid Channels
- **Google Ads** - Search and display advertising
- **Social Media Ads** - Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter
- **Content Sponsorships** - Podcasts, newsletters, blogs
- **Influencer Marketing** - Partner with creators
- **Retargeting** - Re-engage website visitors

### Organic Channels
- **SEO** - Search engine optimization
- **Content Marketing** - Blog, videos, podcasts
- **Social Media** - Organic posts and engagement
- **Community Building** - Forums, Discord, Slack
- **PR** - Press coverage and media mentions

### Partnership Channels
- **Integration Partnerships** - Build on other platforms
- **Co-marketing** - Joint campaigns with partners
- **Affiliate Programs** - Pay for referrals
- **Marketplace Listings** - App stores, directories

## Channel Selection Framework

### Choose Channels Based On:
1. **Your customer** - Where do they spend time?
2. **Your product** - What's the best way to show it?
3. **Your budget** - What can you afford?
4. **Your team** - What skills do you have?
5. **Your stage** - Early stage = organic, later = paid

### Channel Testing Process
1. **Hypothesis** - "Our customers are on LinkedIn"
2. **Test** - Run small campaign or content series
3. **Measure** - Track CAC, conversion rate, LTV
4. **Scale** - Double down on what works
5. **Optimize** - Improve performance continuously

## B2B Acquisition Strategies

### Outbound Sales
- **Cold email** - Personalized, value-first approach
- **LinkedIn outreach** - Connect and message prospects
- **Cold calling** - For high-ticket products
- **Sales development reps (SDRs)** - Dedicated outbound team

### Inbound Marketing
- **SEO content** - Rank for keywords customers search
- **Webinars** - Educational content that builds trust
- **Case studies** - Show how you solve problems
- **Free tools** - Lead magnets (calculators, templates)

### Account-Based Marketing (ABM)
- **Target specific accounts** - High-value customers
- **Personalized campaigns** - Custom content per account
- **Multi-channel approach** - Email, ads, direct mail
- **Sales-marketing alignment** - Coordinated outreach

## B2C Acquisition Strategies

### Social Media
- **Platform-specific content** - TikTok vs. Instagram vs. LinkedIn
- **Influencer partnerships** - Micro and macro influencers
- **User-generated content** - Encourage sharing
- **Paid social ads** - Highly targeted campaigns

### Content Marketing
- **Video content** - YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels
- **Blog content** - SEO-optimized articles
- **Podcasts** - Guest appearances or own show
- **Newsletters** - Regular valuable content

### Referral Programs
- **Incentivize sharing** - Discounts, credits, rewards
- **Make it easy** - One-click sharing
- **Track and reward** - Attribution system
- **Examples**: Dropbox, Uber, Airbnb

## Product-Led Growth

### Free Tier Strategy
- **Freemium model** - Free version with paid upgrade
- **Free trial** - Time-limited full access
- **Self-service** - No sales needed to start
- **In-product upgrades** - Upgrade prompts in app

### Viral Mechanisms
- **Sharing features** - Built into product
- **Network effects** - More users = more value
- **Collaboration** - Invite teammates, friends
- **Examples**: Slack, Notion, Calendly

## Acquisition Funnel

### Awareness Stage
- **Top of funnel** - Broad content, ads
- **Goal**: Get noticed by potential customers
- **Metrics**: Impressions, reach, brand awareness

### Interest Stage
- **Middle of funnel** - Educational content, demos
- **Goal**: Build interest and trust
- **Metrics**: Engagement, time on site, content downloads

### Consideration Stage
- **Bottom of funnel** - Comparisons, case studies
- **Goal**: Help them evaluate your solution
- **Metrics**: Demo requests, pricing page views

### Purchase Stage
- **Conversion** - Sign up, buy, subscribe
- **Goal**: Close the deal
- **Metrics**: Conversion rate, CAC, revenue

## Measuring Acquisition

### Key Metrics
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)** - Cost to acquire customer
- **Conversion rate** - % of visitors who become customers
- **Channel attribution** - Which channels drive customers
- **LTV:CAC ratio** - Should be > 3:1
- **Payback period** - Time to recover CAC

### Tracking Setup
- **UTM parameters** - Tag all links with source
- **Analytics** - Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude
- **CRM** - Track leads through pipeline
- **Attribution** - First-touch, last-touch, multi-touch

## Common Mistakes

1. **Spreading too thin** - Focus on 1-2 channels that work
2. **No tracking** - Can't optimize what you don't measure
3. **Ignoring retention** - Acquisition is expensive, retention is free
4. **Wrong channel fit** - Not where your customers are
5. **Giving up too early** - Channels take time to optimize
6. **Copying competitors** - Find your unique angle
7. **No experimentation** - Always be testing new channels

## Optimization Tips

- **Start small** - Test with small budget before scaling
- **Double down on winners** - Invest more in what works
- **Kill losers fast** - Stop spending on underperforming channels
- **Improve conversion** - Better landing pages, CTAs, messaging
- **Retarget** - Re-engage visitors who didn't convert
- **Automate** - Use tools to scale successful processes
